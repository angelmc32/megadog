GORDUKI

Quest for the Stolen Wubba

This is a game about Gorduki, an English Bulldog

King Gremlin stole her Golden Wubba.

Bad idea.

Move with the arrows, select attack with 1, 2 or 3

Get the wubba in every stage. Collect items for points!

Attack with space (you can charge your shot)

Eliminate the enemies. They take advantage of some attacks!

Eliminate the enemies, get the Wubba and then head to the right for next level.
